ADULT SIMPLIFIED RENEWAL PASSPORT APPLICATION
for eligible Canadians applying in Canada or the USA
A. WHO MAY USE THIS FORM?

Important notice: We recommend that you not finalize your travel plans until you receive the requested passport.

Note: If this is your first application for a Canadian passport, you must apply for a new passport and complete form PPTC 153, Adult General Passport Application.

To renew your Canadian passport, you need the following from your most recent passport:

  • passport number;
  • date of issue;
  • date of expiry.

The passport must have:

  • been issued in the last 15 years;
  • been valid for 5 or 10 years;
  • been issued when you were 16 years of age or older on the date you applied;
  • the same name, date of birth, and place of birth on your new application;
  • matches the current information on your most recent passport; or
  • the same gender identifier on your new application, unless you are requesting a change.

If your current passport does not meet the above requirements, you must apply for a new passport and complete form PPTC 153, Adult General Passport Application.

B. REQUIREMENTS CHECKLIST
  • Both pages of the application form completed and signed within the last twelve (12) months
  • Two (2) identical and unaltered passport photos taken within the last six (6) months. No signature is required on the photos
  • Include any Canadian passport issued to you that is still valid. Complete form PPTC 203 - Declaration concerning a lost, stolen, inaccessible, damaged or found Canadian travel document if the most recent passport is still valid and has been lost, stolen, damaged or found.

Additional documents or information may be requested in support of this application. If you are contacted by the Passport Program to provide information or documentation, you must communicate with us as soon as possible. If the required information is not provided, your application may be refused.

C. FEE AND PERIOD OF VALIDITY

Where the application is made in Canada:

5-year validity CAN$120
10-year validity CAN$160

Where the application is made from the USA:

5-year validity CAN$190
10-year validity CAN$260

The passport is valid for 5 or 10 years depending on the fee paid. The fee is non-refundable and is payable in Canadian funds only.

Methods of payment:

For in-person applications: Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), prepaid card (embossed only), Debit card (Interac), Visa Debit, MasterCard Debit, Virtual Visa Debit and Interac e-Transfer. Cash and personal cheques are not accepted.

For mail-in applications: Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), prepaid card (embossed only), Debit card (Interac), Visa Debit, MasterCard Debit, Virtual Visa Debit and Interac e-Transfer. Cash and personal cheques are not accepted.
